## Recognition

Words like *level by level*, *nearest first*, or *minimum number of edges* usually point to breadth-first search.

## Optimal Solution

Process exactly the queue size present at the beginning of each round. Those nodes make one level.

## Common Mistakes

Do not loop over a changing queue length; capture the level size first.
